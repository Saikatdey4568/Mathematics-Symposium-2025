Venue & Directions

Information about the conference location and how to get there

Conference Location

The Mathematics Department Annual Talks 2025 will be held at the Science Building on the main university campus.

Getting Here

There are several convenient ways to reach the Mathematics Department:

Public Transport

  • Bus: Ranchi-Mesra bus service available from Ranchi city center to BIT Mesra campus gate.
  • Auto-rickshaws and taxis are also readily available from Ranchi city to BIT Mesra campus.

By Car

  • From Ranchi: Take NH33 towards Tata/Jamshedpur. BIT Mesra is located approximately 15 km from Ranchi on NH33.
  • From Jamshedpur: Take NH33 towards Ranchi. BIT Mesra is located approximately 115 km from Jamshedpur.

Parking

Visitor parking is available in Lot P3, adjacent to the Science Building. Faculty members can use their regular parking passes.

From the Airport

  • Birsa Munda Airport (Ranchi) is about 20 km from BIT Mesra campus.
  • Taxi: A taxi ride from the airport to BIT Mesra takes about 30-35 minutes and costs approximately ₹600-800.
  • Pre-paid taxi services are available at the airport terminal.

Campus Facilities

During the conference, attendees will have access to various campus facilities to enhance their experience.

Wi-Fi Access

Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the venue. Students and faculty can use their regular campus Wi-Fi credentials.

Dining Options

The University Cafeteria will be open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM throughout the conference. Additionally, there are several cafés and restaurants within walking distance of the venue.

Library Access

The Mathematics Department Library will have extended hours (8:00 AM - 10:00 PM) during the event. Students can access it using their student ID cards as usual.
