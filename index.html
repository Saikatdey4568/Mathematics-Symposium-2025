About the Event

Welcome to the annual Mathematics Department Talks, a three-day conference bringing together mathematicians, researchers, and students to explore cutting-edge developments in the field of mathematics.

This year's event features distinguished speakers from around the world, covering topics ranging from pure mathematics to applied mathematical sciences and interdisciplinary applications.
